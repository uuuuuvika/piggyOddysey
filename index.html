PIGGY'S ODYSSEY

In this game, you play an alien pig who is on a mission to rescue all of the pigs on Earth! The pigs have been captured by humans and are being held in farms and slaughterhouses. You must use your alien powers to free them!

press SHIFT to teleport a piggy to your planet (you have just ONE portal at a time and it's NOT active immidiately). when there are no more piggies left you WON! (don't let the butchers enter the portal or catch you!)
